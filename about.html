About us

Our commitment

At Yarn yarn yarn, we have a deep appreciation for the art of knitting and a strong commitment to the welfare of our sheep. Our small webshop is dedicated to providing you with the finest, ethically sourced yarns, where the well-being of our sheep is at the heart of everything we do.
We believe that happy and well-cared-for sheep produce the highest quality wool, and we take pride in ensuring their comfort and health. When you shop with us, you're not just purchasing yarn; you're supporting a sustainable and compassionate approach to the world of knitting. Join us in celebrating the warmth, comfort, and creativity that knitting brings, while also embracing our love for these wonderful creatures.
